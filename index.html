About Me

My Name is Alexis Tanner, but my friends call me Lex! I was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, where I still currently reside. A few of my many hobbies are eating, sleeping, shopping, and traveling. My next big trip is going to be to Brazil and I am so elated.

I currently work for Best Buy's very own Geek Squad, as a Subject Matter Expert. I have been with Geek Squad for almost three years and I learn something new everyday. My job is exciting because it allows me to exercise my problem solving and critical thinking skills with real-live scenarios. Soon, I plan to start a tech company, mainly focusing my services on installations, web/program design, advertising, software troubleshooting, and technical support.
